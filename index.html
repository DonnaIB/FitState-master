Fit State Training

Fit State Training is a personal training studio in the heart of Brighton & Hove, specialising in 1-2-1 and small group training sessions. Every one is different, we create a bespoke plan to make sure you get the most effect programme, reach your fitness goals and have some fun!

Your body is the most important thing you own, look after it well and you will reap the rewards. Take the first small step and get in touch today and book a free introductory session.
